## UX Guidelines (bullets only)

- Instant feedback loops: show immediate responses (micro-animations/loading states) so users know the app is working.
- One clear action: guide users to a single obvious next step; reduce decision fatigue.
- Frictionless interactions: make actions easy to tap/click; minimize effort for primary tasks.
- Emotional design: use visuals/motion to evoke the intended feeling; match pacing to the moment.
- Visible social activity: surface activity/participation cues to convey momentum and encourage engagement.
